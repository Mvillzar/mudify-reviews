Who Is Mudify?

Mudify is an online off-road and overlanding accessories retailer headquartered in Miami, Florida. They specialize in suspension systems, lift kits, armor, recovery gear, and camping equipment for 4x4 vehicles of all platforms.

Authorized dealer for the industry's leading brands. Offers vehicle-specific build guidance, financing through Affirm & Snap Finance, and discounts for military and first responders.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions answered based on 445+ verified reviews and brand data.

Is Mudify a legitimate and trustworthy company?+
Yes. Mudify is a legitimate off-road accessories retailer based in Miami, FL with 445+ verified reviews and a 4.3/5 average rating. They are an authorized dealer for Old Man Emu, Fox, Bilstein, King Shocks, Dobinsons, and more.
How is Mudify's customer service quality?+
Mudify's customer service is consistently their highest-rated attribute. Reviews praise responsive communication, knowledgeable staff, and honest advice. Team members Samantha and Eddie have been specifically named in positive reviews. A minority of reviews cite slow updates during supply chain delays, but most issues are ultimately resolved.
How fast does Mudify ship, and is packaging reliable?+
Most customers report fast shipping with well-packaged deliveries arriving in perfect condition. Delays have been noted on specific suspension components during supply chain disruptions. Confirm live stock availability — especially for time-sensitive builds — before placing orders.
What off-road brands does Mudify carry?+
Mudify carries Old Man Emu (OME), Fox Shocks, Bilstein, King Shocks, Dobinsons, BDS Suspension, Icon Vehicle Dynamics, ARB, Warn Winches, and many more across suspension, armor, recovery, wheels, and overlanding categories.
Does Mudify offer financing or special discounts?+
Yes. Mudify offers flexible financing through Affirm and Snap Finance. They also provide discounts for military personnel and first responders. Contact them at (786) 699-8829 for current promotions.
How does Mudify handle returns and product issues?+
Based on reviews, Mudify generally resolves product issues through their customer service team. Calling directly at (786) 699-8829 yields faster results than email. Most issues are resolved, though times vary from same-day to several weeks.
